About Us

Who we are!

iTRAFFIC is a traveller information service provided to the Motor Transport and Traffic Directorate. It provides traffic conditions information, road works, congestion and traffic alert information for Accra and other regions. iTRAFFIC informs travellers in these regions about current conditions on the national roads, with more detailed information for the urban areas where it has real-time congestion information.

iTRAFFIC is a free service to travellers in Ghana. It is implementing a state-of-the-art Freeway Management System in major urban areas throughout the country. Information from this system enables it to better manage traffic and incidents, provides vital information to emergency responders, and supports better road and traffic condition information to be provided to travellers. iTRAFFIC information is monitored and updated by operators 24 hours per day, 7 days per week from the Traffic Management Centres in Accra, Kumasi and Cape Coast.

Our Mandate

iTraffic's mandate is to promote good driving and traffic-free road standards in the country and ensure the use of road worthy vehicles on the roads and other public places.

Our Vision

To be a reputable application with locally accepted standards for driver and and road traffic regulations

Our Mission

Building strong community of worthy personnels to promote road safety and traffic-free roads and environmental sustainability, while pursuing integrity, excellence, professionalism and reliability in service delivery.
